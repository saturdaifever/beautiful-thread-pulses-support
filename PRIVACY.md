# Beautiful Thread Pulses Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-13

Beautiful Thread Pulses is a local Stream Deck plugin. It does not operate a separate cloud service, create a product account, sell personal data, or intentionally collect analytics.

The plugin reads local ChatGPT App information needed to provide its features, including the installed command catalog, shortcut status, and Codex thread state. It may pass prompts, task instructions, thread identifiers, and approval decisions to the local ChatGPT or Codex installation when the user invokes the corresponding control.

Stream Deck stores action settings locally. Do not place secrets in an action setting unless you are comfortable with that value being stored by Stream Deck on your Mac.

Any information that ChatGPT or Codex sends to OpenAI or another configured service is governed by the user's ChatGPT, Codex, and service configuration and by the applicable provider policies. Beautiful Thread Pulses does not add a separate remote data path.

Diagnostic logs may be written locally on the Mac to support troubleshooting. They are not uploaded automatically. A user chooses whether to share them when requesting support.

For privacy or support questions, use the public support repository linked from the Elgato Marketplace listing.
